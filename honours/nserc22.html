NSERC USRA, 2022
In mid-April of 2022, I was informed that I was selected for the Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council's (NSERC) Undergraduate Student Research Award (USRA). I am so incredibly excited to get the opportunity to conduct meaningful research with a professor at Ontario Tech. For this project, I will be working under the supervision of Dr. Jane Breen, researching the application of network theory to solve problems relating to infodemiology. I am thrilled to be given the opportunity to delve into research in the realm of mathematics, which has not been my focus thus far in my research career developmental journey.
