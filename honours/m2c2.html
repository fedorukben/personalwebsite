MEDLIFE Movement Case Competition (M2C2)
On May 7, 2022, along with a team from the Northern Shores Innovation Institute, I created a solution to address the ongoing healthcare crisis in Peru. We proposed and designed Edu-Act Peru, a three-layered system to assist with the transport of patients to and from medical centres. Our solution encorporates HealthDecks, HealthBeacons and MediLink. The team won the ICUBE Third Place Award at the one-day competition.
