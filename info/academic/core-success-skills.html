Core Success Skills
I completed the Core Success Skills microcredential program, offered in collaboration between Ontario Tech University and the TD Ready Commitment. The 21st Century Career Ready Pathway in the Core Success Skills program consists of key transferrable skills that are validated through a variety of different opportunities and experiences. Each module consisted of a reading, a video ( often TEDx talks), and an assessment. The assessments were either as-planned or as-lived. The modules were completed in eight subcategories, as follows:
  • Innovation and Problem Solving
  • Self Awareness
  • Cultural Fluency
  • Information Literacy
  • Creative and Flexible Thinking
  • Self Efficacy
  • Cooperative Collaboration
  • Oral Communication
