Secondary School
I attended Sir Winston Churchill Collegiate and Vocational Institute for my former two years of secondary school. After the school's closure, I moved to Superior Collegiate and Vocational Institute. I received International Baccalaureate Diploma Certificates in Higher-Level English and Standard-Level Mathematics, receiving a 5 in each subject. I graduated from Superior Collegiate and Vocational Institute, through the International Baccalaureate Programme in June 2020. I also received my Ontario Secondary School Diploma, in supplement to my International Baccalaureate certificates. I was deemed an Ontario Scholar by the Ministry of Education, in November 2020.
Undergraduate Studies

I am studying for a Bachelor of Science degree in Integrated Mathematics and Computer Science. My expected graduation should occur at the end of the academic year of 2024. I plan on pursuing an undergraduate thesis in my final year of study.
