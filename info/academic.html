I have truly excelled in my educational experiences. I attended Sir Winston Churchill Collegiate and Vocational Institute for my former two years of secondary school. After the school's closure, I moved to Superior Collegiate and Vocational Institute. I graduated from Superior Collegiate and Vocational Institute, through the International Baccalaureate Programme in June 2020. I also received my Ontario Secondary School Diploma, in supplement to my International Baccalaureate certificates. I was deemed an Ontario Scholar by the Ministry of Education, in November 2020.
