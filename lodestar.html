The Lodestar
The Benjamin D. Fedoruk Lodestar is currently in the early stages of publication. This is essentially a newsletter posted by myself, not on a consistent basis, but instead whenever I have thoughts. The main purpose of this is to show my intellectual growth as a thinker. I know that I will be wrong on the Lodestar. I know I will change my opinions. But I want to show you that I have the ability to critically think. And that I have the ability to learn. The Lodestar is available through Substack, for free. The Lodestar will be philosophical, and will consist of many non-computer science and non-mathematical posts.
